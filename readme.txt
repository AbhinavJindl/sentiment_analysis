-------------How to run-------------
$python3 run.py <expt no (2-5)>

In order to change the training data use:
$python3 preprocessing.py
(the results in the pdf may then vary from new results)

-------------To change variables-------
In order to change the number of features, instances or forest trees and its features, change the variable names at the top of preprocessing.py

pickle is used to save the training data.
